About me

I am currently a Postdoctoral researcher working with Hélène Parisot-Dupuis and Fabien Mery within the Département Aérodynamique, énergétique et Propulsion at ISAE-SUPAERO Toulouse. In my research, I aim to reconstruct aeroacoustic sources using bayesian approaches.

Between October 2020 and April 2024, I was a PhD student in the Modélisation Stochastique et Numérique team, under the supervision of Stephanie Lohrengel and Marion Darbas. My thesis focused on the coupling of two brain imaging modalities: electroencephalography and diffuse optical tomography. The aim of this work was to propose a model of neurovascular coupling, source localization in electroencephalography and parameter identification in optical tomography.
